Where is bagain

Born on August 4th 1991

Jessica entered this world bright eyed and bushy tailed. Her mother Jocelyne Montas imagrated from Hait,
and raised 5 great kids. Jessica was her 4th child. Jessica also had dreams of working in the medical field, like her mom.
Jessica attened SouthTech Academy, there she took classes for the medical science graduated with Personal Care Assistants (PCA),
Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA), and Home Health Aid certificates in 2009.

Life Goes On

After high school Jessica worked as a Home Health Aid and CNA for multiple companies.
Still Jessica knew this was not the path she wanted to stay in for a career. In 2012 Jessica started looking at other options
in the healthcare world. She came across Registered Nurse (RN) and started looking and applying for schools in the area.
In 2013 Jessica got accepted into Dade Medical College. There she took all the coruses needed to get her Associate's degree in Nursing.
In October 2014 Jessica found out that she was pregnate with her first child. Still that did not stop Jessica she contuined the coruse
and graduated in Novomber 2015. In January 2016 Jessica when to take her NCLEX-RN exam. She still tells me to this day how
nervous she was and even though she was the last examinee in the room she still pressed hard and PASSED the NCLEX-RN to officaling be come an RN.

Motherhood
